About us

Some words about us

Adhara care centre has been established under Adhara Trust and has been operating in shivamogga from last 15 years. The inmates here are served with food, accomodation and medication.

If there is a person in your family, whom you cannot look after, we take care of him/her. He/she may be mentally ill, physically incapable or may be under phychiatric treatment due to intake of alcohal etc. Dippression or other disorders might have made him/her disable to take care of him/herself.

As everybody is employed during these days, family may not be able to look of elderly people. We are doing our best to serve them with love and affection in a homely atmosphere.
